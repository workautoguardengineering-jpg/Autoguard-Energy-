About Autoguard Energy

Who We Are

Autoguard Energy Limited is an engineering and industrial procurement company. We connect industries directly with Original Equipment Manufacturers, or OEMs.

Through our OEM partnerships and years of technical procurement experience, we help mining, power, manufacturing, food processing, and other industrial sectors get genuine replacement parts and equipment quickly, without the usual back and forth.

Our Mission

To reduce equipment downtime, eliminate the risks that come with counterfeit components, and simplify industrial procurement across Zambia and the region.

Our Approach

We act as the trusted link between asset owners and OEMs worldwide. That means handling specification, sourcing, logistics, and delivery, with real technical expertise behind every step.

The Challenge We Solve

Industrial procurement is slower and riskier than it should be

Common Industry Problems

  • Long, unpredictable procurement cycles
  • Counterfeit or incompatible parts entering the supply chain
  • Unplanned equipment downtime from delayed sourcing
  • Juggling multiple, unverified suppliers
  • Limited or no direct access to OEMs

How Autoguard Energy Solves It

  • Direct lines to OEMs shorten sourcing timelines
  • Every part is genuine, verified, and traceable to source
  • Proactive sourcing reduces unplanned downtime
  • One accountable partner for specification through delivery
  • Established global OEM partnerships, on your behalf
